# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People using this app are pairs of friends who want a lightweight way to keep score in ongoing disputes, challenges, or friendly rivalries. They usually arrive via invite link, check the current match state quickly, cast a daily vote, and review totals or recent history without extra ceremony.

## Product Purpose

Contyplacar exists to make one-on-one scoring simple, social, and repeatable. It should help two people start a match, vote on daily scores, track totals over time, and revisit the history of the dispute with minimal friction.

## Brand Personality

Simple, warm, trustworthy. The voice should feel friendly and clear, with a small competitive edge, but never aggressive or overly playful.

## Anti-references

Avoid enterprise dashboard visuals, noisy gamification, neon gaming aesthetics, childish stickers, and cluttered admin-panel layouts. Do not make it feel like finance software, a sports broadcast graphic, or a generic SaaS template.

## Design Principles

- Make the current action obvious at a glance.
- Keep the social loop lightweight: invite, vote, compare, repeat.
- Show totals and history as support for the match, not as the main event.
- Favor clarity and momentum over feature density.
- Keep competition friendly and low-stakes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Support keyboard navigation, strong text contrast, clear focus states, and status cues that do not rely on color alone. Treat reduced motion as a default-friendly option if animation is introduced later.
